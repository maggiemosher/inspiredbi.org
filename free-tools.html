A Note on Privacy and "Agentic" Behavior

Privacy Disclaimer While platforms like Formative and PlayLab.ai explicitly cite FERPA and COPPA compliance, "compliance" often depends on how your specific district sets up the integration. Always check with your district's IT department to see if they have a Data Privacy Agreement (DPA) on file for these vendors before rostering students.
Agentic vs. Chatbot "Agentic" tools (like those you can build in PlayLab) can be programmed to follow a series of steps — such as "check the student's answer against this specific rubric, then provide a hint instead of the answer." Basic chatbots, by contrast, generally respond to individual prompts without the ability to "manage" the session workflow autonomously.
Data Usage As a best practice, assume that unless a tool specifically states that data is not used to train their models, it might be. If you are using these tools to analyze student work, always opt for platforms that offer "enterprise" or "district" privacy settings where data remains siloed to your school/district.
