Compare Platforms: Generative vs. Agentic AI

What does Google, Anthropic, Microsoft, and Apple offer in Generative and Agentic AI — and how do they compare for educators?

The Full Picture

AI Ecosystem Matrix — Generative vs. Agentic

Every major tech company now offers both generative AI (responds to prompts) and agentic AI (takes autonomous action). Understanding where each product falls on this spectrum helps educators choose the right tool for each job.

AI Paradigm Type Anthropic (Claude) Google Microsoft Apple
Conversational AI Generative Claude Chat Gemini Microsoft Copilot Apple Writing Tools / ChatGPT integration
Coding & Engineering Agentic Claude Code Vertex AI Agents / IDX GitHub Copilot Workspace Xcode Swift Assist

OS / Desktop Automation Agentic Claude Cowork Project Astra / Android Windows Copilot Vision Siri On-Screen Awareness

Key insight: The line between generative and agentic is blurring rapidly. What matters for educators is whether the AI requires you to initiate every action (generative) or can complete multi-step goals autonomously (agentic). Both have important educational uses — the right choice depends on the task and your comfort with AI autonomy.

Google AI

The Google AI Ecosystem

Google offers the most comprehensive AI ecosystem for education — from free tools accessible with any Gmail account to enterprise-grade agentic AI for large institutions. Their FERPA compliance and educational pricing make them the safest starting point for K–12 deployment.

Google AI Ecosystem

From free Gemini to Workspace Studio Agents — five products that span the full generative-to-agentic spectrum.

Gemini

Generative

Google's flagship conversational AI. Free at gemini.google.com. Integrated into Workspace Education tiers. The most FERPA-safe option when accessed through an educational Google account.

Vertex AI Agents / IDX

Agentic

Enterprise-grade agent development platform. Project IDX is Google's cloud-based development environment with AI agents for coding. Best for technically advanced educators building custom solutions.

NotebookLM

Generative

AI research assistant grounded in your uploaded documents. Free with any Google account. Generates quizzes, study guides, and Audio Overview podcasts from curriculum materials. The safest AI for student-facing use.

Gemini Gems

Agentic

Custom AI agents built in Gemini Advanced. Educators can create "Gems" with specific system prompts and knowledge bases — like a permanent AI teaching assistant with your curriculum baked in. Requires Google AI Pro for Education.

Project Astra / Workspace Studio

Agentic

Google's most advanced agentic AI — Workspace Studio lets you build no-code agents that monitor Google Drive folders, process documents, and trigger automations. Requires Google AI Pro for Education + IT configuration.

Microsoft AI

The Microsoft AI Ecosystem

Microsoft integrates AI across its entire product suite through Copilot — from free web access to enterprise-grade agentic workflows in Microsoft 365 Education. FedRAMP High certified, making it the preferred choice for federal education grants and sensitive research.

Microsoft AI Ecosystem

Copilot embedded across Word, Teams, and OneNote — plus Copilot Studio for custom agent building.

Microsoft Copilot

Generative

Microsoft's flagship AI assistant — embedded in Word, Outlook, PowerPoint, Teams, and OneNote. Available in M365 Education A1 (free) through A5 tiers. Strong FERPA compliance when accessed through educational accounts.

GitHub Copilot Workspace

Agentic

100% free for verified educators through GitHub Global Campus. Reads entire codebases, proposes multi-file plans, and writes code autonomously. Ideal for educators building XR simulations or data analysis scripts without traditional coding skills.

Copilot Pages / Loop

Generative

Collaborative AI workspaces in Microsoft Teams and Loop. Like NotebookLM but built into Teams — teams can collaboratively query AI against shared documents. Good for faculty working groups and department-level curriculum design.

Copilot Studio

Agentic

Low-code platform for building custom AI agents on Microsoft infrastructure. Educators can build agents that integrate with SharePoint, Teams, and Outlook without coding. Enterprise-grade, FedRAMP compliant. Requires M365 premium licensing.

Windows Copilot Vision

Agentic

Copilot's evolving OS-level awareness — can see what's on your screen and assist across applications. Currently in preview. Will allow educators to dictate tasks across applications while Copilot executes them visually. Privacy controls are a key consideration.

Apple AI

The Apple AI Ecosystem

Apple's approach to AI prioritizes on-device processing and privacy above all else — most Apple Intelligence features never send data to a server. This makes Apple the strongest privacy option for K–12 environments, though capabilities are more limited than Google or Microsoft.

Apple AI Ecosystem

On-device AI first — Apple Intelligence processes most requests locally, keeping student data off the cloud by default.

Apple Writing Tools

Generative

Built into iOS 18 and macOS Sequoia — available in any text field across the operating system. Proofread, rewrite, summarize, and generate text without leaving your app. Fully on-device for most tasks — no data leaves the device. No account required, no cost beyond the device.

Xcode Swift Assist

Agentic

AI coding assistant integrated into Xcode for Swift and Swift UI development. Helps educators building iOS apps or Apple platform tools for educational XR and accessibility applications. Designed for on-device Apple development specifically.

Apple Notes Integration

Generative

Apple Intelligence can summarize and organize notes, generate action lists from meeting notes, and create Smart Reply suggestions in Mail. Useful for teacher productivity — meeting notes, parent communication drafts, and lesson ideation — all processed on-device.

Siri Shortcuts / App Intents

Agentic

Siri Shortcuts and App Intents allow educators to build automated workflows across Apple apps — trigger sequences like "When I say 'morning routine,' open attendance sheet, start timer, and send good morning email to class." Agentic at the operating system level.

Siri On-Screen Awareness

Agentic

Siri can now see and understand what's on your screen (with permission) and take actions based on visual context. Ask Siri to "add this event to my calendar" while looking at an event flyer, or "share this assignment rubric with my class group." Privacy-first — Apple cannot access this data.

Anthropic (Claude)

The Anthropic / Claude Ecosystem

Anthropic's Claude is widely regarded as the most thoughtful and nuanced conversational AI — with a particularly strong approach to safety and ethics. Claude Cowork (formerly Claude Computer Use) brings agentic capabilities directly to your desktop, working with your local files autonomously.

Claude AI Ecosystem

From conversational Claude to autonomous Claude Cowork — Anthropic's safety-first approach to AI for educators.

Claude Chat

Generative

Anthropic's flagship conversational AI — widely praised for nuanced, thoughtful responses and strong ethics. Available at claude.ai. Free tier available; paid plans from $20/month. Strong for academic writing, grant narratives, lesson planning, and complex analysis.

Claude Code

Agentic

Anthropic's agentic coding assistant — operates as a terminal-based agent that can read, write, and execute code across your entire project. Ideal for educators building research tools, data pipelines, or educational applications who want a highly capable autonomous developer.

Claude Projects

Generative

Persistent AI workspaces where you upload documents and maintain conversation context across sessions. Like NotebookLM but more conversational and flexible. Upload your curriculum, syllabi, and rubrics — Claude maintains context across all future conversations in that project.

Claude Skills / API Agents

Agentic

Custom AI agents built on the Claude API — for educators and institutions with technical capacity. Integrates with any system via API. PlayLab.ai and other educator platforms are built on the Claude API, making Claude's intelligence accessible without coding.

Claude Cowork

Agentic

Anthropic's desktop agentic AI — works directly in your file system, writing files, organizing folders, and executing multi-step tasks autonomously. Requires Claude Pro/Max/Team/Enterprise ($20+/month) and the Claude Desktop app. The most capable local agentic AI for individual educators.
