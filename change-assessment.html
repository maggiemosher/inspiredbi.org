About These Tools

These interactive visualizations were built from materials produced by the KU PRISM Project in partnership with KSDE's "Balanced Assessments with KAP" professional development series (Fall 2025). They show Kansas ELA teachers exactly how the six standard domains connect to each other, how any single standard grows in complexity from kindergarten through grade 12, how academic standards map to high-value careers, and how mini tests, interims, and the KAP summative form one coherent instructional system. The citations tool traces every data point back to its originating source document.
